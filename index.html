Founded with Veteran Values

Our Story

Care Home by RNs was founded in 2006 by nurses and healthcare professionals who saw a gap in senior care. They believed that quality elder care required more than good intentions — it required clinical expertise, personal accountability, and genuine compassion.

What started as a single assisted living home has grown into a comprehensive continuum of care, serving hundreds of seniors and their families across Sacramento and Lincoln. Yet we've maintained our core mission: providing care that honors dignity, respects autonomy, and is grounded in clinical excellence.

Today, over 70% of Care Home by RNs is owned by shareholding nurses and caregivers — the first assisted living organization to offer ownership shares to qualified caregivers. This unique model aligns our financial success with your loved one's well-being.
